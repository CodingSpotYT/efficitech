About Us

Before Efficitech was created by CodingSpotYT, he made SchoolOrganized. A website that makes bookmarks easier and more customizable. After making it, he realized that a lot of today's apps are unproductive or hard to use. So he created Efficitech, the company that improves day-to-day apps to make them better. He soon hired the help of GITGUtter123 who is also a member of Efficitech. Together we have created many notable things like SchoolOrganized, DeckedOut, ClipNote, and TabVault.
