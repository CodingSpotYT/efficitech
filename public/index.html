About Us

Before Efficitech was created by Gowtham, he made SchoolOrganized. A website that makes bookmarks easier and more customizable. After making it, he realized that a lot of today's apps are unproductive or hard to use. So he created Efficitech, the company that improves day-to-day apps to make them better. He soon hired the help of Ramis, who is also a member of Efficitech. Together, they have created many notable things like SchoolOrganized, DeckedOut, ClipNote, and TabVault.

SchoolOrganized

Bookmarks Organizer

View Project

Traditional browser bookmarks are cluttered and difficult to navigate. SchoolOrganized is designed to fix that with a clean, customizable, and intuitive system that makes organizing and accessing your saved links effortless. Whether for school, research, or personal use, it keeps everything neatly structured, so you can find what you need instantly. No more scrolling through endless folders—SchoolOrganized brings order to your bookmarks.
